Daily notes for 2025-08-22.

I want to get better at committing. My whole life, I've been really good a hyperfocusing on a particular task for a few weeks or sometimes months. But, inevitably, over time, I lose interests in improving / keeping up that tasks and I switch to a new task. Or, I think that what I am doing is useless so get stuck in a loop of switching back and forth between two strategies, which is inefficient.

It's unfortunate that as finite beings, we have finite lives. Thus, our time here is absolutely precious. To make better use of my time, I need to commit to a few habits which will make my life a bit easier. Such as:

  • Sleeping consistently from 2 am to 8 am (Helps with everything).
    • A big part of this is not getting distracted and getting addicted to some show/audiobook before bed.
    • Additionally, I need to record and keep track of my sleep statistics every day.
  • Washing my glasses, hygeine, etc. (Helps study for longer)
  • Stick to one and ONLY one textbook for self-studying and not switching back and forth between textbooks.
  • Writing in this blog
  • Taking time each week to work on my hobbies (drawing, programming, music, etc.)
  • Exercise every day, even if it's just a short jog.
  • Make sure to eat at regular times. Eating at a regular time is a higher priority than even homework.
  • Meditate for at least 15 mins every day.
General principle: Consistency matters more than perfection. This is hard for me to accept, being a perfectionist, but I think it is absolutely true.
